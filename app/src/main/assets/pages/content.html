Preface

There is no single mobile application for the preparation of judicial services examination. Therefore, the need for an application which is exhaustive, comprehensive, and lucid has been felt since long by those who are judicial aspirants and practicing lawyers. The present work is a humble attempt in that direction and I am very happy in placing before the law students and young advocates this mobile application.

This mobile application is a collection of no. of central as well as state acts, and important Legal maxims, major landmark judgments, Practice questions(subject wise), previous year exam question papers etc. The practice questions contain questions asked in various preliminary examinations. Our Endeavour is to enable and equip the aspirants with the essential knowledge required for the preparation of judicial exams. We have made sincere efforts to make the present work much more informative and useful.

I am very grateful to my parents Dr. Jatinder Gulati(Father) and Smt. Rashmi Gulati(Mother) for their sacrifice and co-operation without which the creation of this application was not possible. I am also thankful to my friends, mentors, admirers for appreciating the work and making several suggestions from time to time.
